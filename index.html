About My Hobby: Photography

Photography is the art, application, and practice of creating durable images. For me, it is not just about clicking pictures; it is about capturing emotions, stories, and frozen moments in time that can be cherished forever.

To learn more about the technical aspects of photography, you can visit the National Geographic Photography Hub.

Why I Love Photography

  • It allows me to express my creativity visually.
  • It changes the way I see the world, making me appreciate small details.
  • It helps me connect with new people and explore breathtaking locations.
  • It preserves beautiful memories with friends and family.
